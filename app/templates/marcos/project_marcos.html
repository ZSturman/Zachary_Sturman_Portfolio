Examining the Effectiveness of Tip Redistributing in the Casino Industry

A Data-Driven Approach Revisiting The Impact of Current Tipping Practices

Goal

  • The objective of this data analysis case study is to evaluate the current tip pooling system in casinos and propose a new system that addresses the issues of unfairness and one-sidedness.
  • The focus is on the redistribution of tips between Blackjack and Craps dealers, with the aim of providing a livable hourly wage for all dealers.

Outcome

  • The results of this study indicate that the current tip pooling system in casinos is unfair to Blackjack dealers who forfeit a portion of their tips to be distributed among Craps dealers, despite earning less than them.
  • I propose a new system based on a sliding-scale approach that redistributes tips based on actual pay. This new system aims to provide a more fair and balanced approach to tip redistribution in the casino industry.

The current redistribution system effectively ensures equitable weekly pay among dealers

...most of the time.

Process

BUSINESS

The practice of redistributing tips among casino dealers has been a contentious issue for some time. In particular, the tip pooling system that is currently in place has been criticized for being unjust and one-sided. Specifically, the system tends to favor Craps dealers over Blackjack dealers.

DATA

Data collected for this study is from September 17, 2021 to September 30, 2021.

ANALYZE

Through careful analysis of this data, opportunities for improving team dynamics and increasing fairness in the distribution of tips among all dealers was discovered.

VISUALIZE

Using JavaScript and Flask the visualizations are easily understood with accessible, interactive data.
