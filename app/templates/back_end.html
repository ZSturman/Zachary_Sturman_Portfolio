Back-end Engineering

What is the role of back-end in web applications?

Back-end architecture is like the behind-the-scenes part of a website or app. Think of it like the kitchen of a restaurant. Just like the kitchen makes the food and keeps it safe, the back-end makes sure that everything on the website or app works and keeps all the important information safe. The back-end is made up of different parts like the place where all the information is stored, the computer that runs the website, and the program that makes everything happen. The front-end, which is what you see on the screen, talks to the back-end to get things done.
