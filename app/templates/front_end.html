What are the components of Front-end Design?

UI (User Interface) design is the visual design and layout of the elements a user interacts with on a website or application. It includes the buttons, forms, and other interactivity elements, as well as the overall look and feel of the interface.

UX (User Experience) design is the overall experience a user has with a website or application, taking into account all aspects of interaction, including the ease of use, accessibility, and the effectiveness of the interface in achieving the user's goals. It encompasses the entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, including branding, design, usability, and function.
