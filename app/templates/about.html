Get To Know Zachary Sturman

As the founder of ZSDynamics, a one-stop-shop for data science based in Denver, Colorado, I bring a unique blend of passion, expertise and experience to the field. My background in storytelling, 3D simulations and scientific data analysis, and my desire to find creative solutions to complex problems, have driven me to start ZSDynamics.

My goal is to help employers navigate the often daunting task of understanding and utilizing the data they have collected, by providing actionable strategies and solutions. I am excited to use my skills and experience to help organizations make the most of their data.

My Story:

What attracted me to data science the most was my skills with Blender, an opensource, 3D animation software which can be used for almost any and every need you may have with data visualization. I knew the math and the software, now I just had to learn the language.

After doing a bit of research I discovered that Python was the language for me. I jumped on YouTube and started watching tutorials from the fundamentals of strings and booleans to neuroscience computation and everything in between. Through plenty of trial and error I created a note-taking and studying strategy that worked for me.

Needing to still bring home a paycheck to survive I switched to part-time as a casino dealer and logged anywhere from 4 to 12 hours a day watching tutorials, reading documentation, creating projects and drinking lot's of coffee. After several months of confusion and pulling out my hair I finally got to a point where I was ready to show the world what I was capable of.

The Intersection of Science, Storytelling, and Animal Advocacy

As a passionate animal lover, I strive to make a positive impact by promoting animal welfare in any way possible. From supporting free-range and humanely raised animal products to exploring the potential of lab-grown meat, I am dedicated to the cause.

But my interests go beyond just animal welfare. I have a fascination with the complexities of science and the seemingly magical world that exists at the quantum level. I am constantly amazed by the strange and fascinating concepts of particles that aren't particles, faster than light communication, and the possibility of time-travel.

I also have a creative side, having pursued a career as a writer and even having my short story published in Aviator magazine back in 2012 then my novel in 2017. My love for storytelling led me to animation, where I created a TV pilot called Greenhorn (viewer discretion advised). With my unique combination of scientific curiosity and creative talent, I am eager to share my story and continue making a positive impact in the world.
